Japanesa Lolita Fashion

Japanese fashion inspired primarily by the clothing and general aesthetics of the Rococo and victorian periods. This fashion movement started in Japan in the 1980's and has evolved greatly since. Nowadays Lolita fashion has grown to be relatively popular all over the world. Relatively popular for an obscure Japanese fashion, that is.

Glossary
Word Meaning
coord short for “coordinate”, a term used to refer to one’s Lolita outfit.
colorway a piece from the same series in a certain color.
j-fashion short for “japanese fashion”. Used to refer to any japanese street fashion style.
loliable portmanteau of “Lolita” and “useable”. Used when an item is not necessarily made for Lolita specifically, but can be used in a coord.
decoden a style of decorating items using rhinestones, cabochons, clay pieces and (optionally) silicone/soft clay piped in a way similar to whipped cream.
